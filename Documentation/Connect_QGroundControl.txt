Connect to QGroundControl
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


This document describes the necessary steps to connect to QGroundControl. 

1) Download QGroundControl from "http://www.qgroundcontrol.org/downloads".
2) Install.
3) Open.
4) Select "Serial link" (Plane icon).
5) Plug in the Xbee ground station in USB port and power up the Maveric autopilot.
6) Select Xbee ground station COM port (E.g: "COM78").
7) Click on "Connect".
8) Done. Enjoy your hobby!
